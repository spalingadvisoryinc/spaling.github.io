Teaching

Advanced Network Security

Designed, authored, and delivered the complete Advanced Network Security curriculum for the University of Alberta's Master of Science in Internetworking program. I retain ownership of the course's intellectual property, including the lectures, laboratories, virtual environments, assignments, assessments, and supporting course materials. The curriculum combines modern cryptography, TLS, browser security, quantum computing, network attacks, segmentation, vulnerability disclosure, ethics, and professional practice into a graduate-level course built around real-world application.
